Complete ORF1 sequences











Evolutionary analysis by Maximum Likelihood method The evolutionary history was inferred by using the Maximum Likelihood method and General Time Reversible model GTR+I+G [1]. The tree is drawn to scale, with branch lengths measured in the number of substitutions per site. Evolutionary analyses were conducted in MEGA X [2].


1. Nei M. and Kumar S. (2000). Molecular Evolution and Phylogenetics. Oxford University Press, New York.


2. Kumar S., Stecher G., Li M., Knyaz C., and Tamura K. (2018). MEGA X: Molecular Evolutionary Genetics Analysis across computing platforms. Molecular Biology and Evolution 35:1547-1549.
